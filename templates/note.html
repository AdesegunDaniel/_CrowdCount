Important Notice

The "CrowdCount" model is designed to detect crowds in images using density maps. It may interpret the background of images containing one, two, or a few individuals as a crowd. Consequently, if an image with only a few people is analyzed, the model's behavior may appear inaccurate. This is due to the training data, which primarily consists of images with dense crowds. Thank you for your understanding.

This is an important notice for the users of the CrowdCount model. Please ensure that you understand the limitations and intended use of the model before proceeding.
